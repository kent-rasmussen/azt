# Text Collection prompts

## Narrative
- Please spend three minutes telling me a story about something that happened to you today.
- Please spend three minutes telling me a story about something that happened to you yesterday.
- Please spend three minutes telling me a story about something that happened to you about a year ago.
- Please spend three minutes telling me a story or fable traditionally known in your culture.

## Proceedure
- Please spend three minutes telling me how to prepare your favorite meal.
- Please spend three minutes telling me how to have a good community meeting.
- Please spend three minutes telling me how to do your job well.

## Hortatory
- Please spend three minutes telling me how to be a good husband.
- Please spend three minutes telling me how to be a good wife.
- Please spend three minutes telling me how to be a good student.

## Law
- Please spend three minutes listing and explaining the most basic rules in your culture.
- Please spend three minutes listing and explaining the most basic rules in your country.
- Please spend three minutes listing and explaining the most basic rules in your family.
