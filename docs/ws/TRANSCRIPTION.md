# Transcription

## Prerequisites
- [General workshop prerequisites](../WORKSHOPS.md#Prerequisites)
- Computer with A-Z+T installed

## Goals
- Decide on second forms for Nouns and for Verbs, and make sure everyone knows how to find either for a given word.
- *Add and Parse Words with Audio* (computer), assuming there is a second form which is morphologically related to your citation form, which confirms part of speech (as noun or verb).
  - Assuming you've done bulk ASR, 
    - you will have buttons to select one of the machine transcriptions for each word; 
    - pick the best one, and 
    - edit it as you like before continuing.
  - When you click "Next", A-Z+T will ask you for the second form for that word.
- *Add Words with Audio* (computer) can be used if there is no such second form. Please be sure to know somehow when you are dealing with a noun, and when you are dealing with a verb, and add the correct part of speech for each word.

## Follow-up Action Items
- Finish transcribing and parsing the Comparative African wordlist.
- Make sure your computer connects to the internet to update GitHub, to keep your data available to your consultant.
